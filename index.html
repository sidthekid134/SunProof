Meet the Founder

Saanvi, Founder of SunProof

Project SunProof was founded by Saanvi, a student from Frankfort, Kentucky, who launched the initiative through the Future Problem Solving competition. What began as an academic project soon evolved into a meaningful mission to make sun safety education more accessible within her community.

Driven by the belief that prevention starts with awareness, Saanvi created Project SunProof to encourage healthier outdoor habits, promote sunscreen use, and provide reliable educational resources about skin health and UV protection.

Today, Project SunProof continues to grow with the goal of helping people of all ages understand the importance of protecting their skin every day.

Why Sun Safety Matters

Your Skin Protects You.
Protect It Back.

Every day, your skin is exposed to ultraviolet (UV) radiation, even on cloudy days. Over time, repeated exposure can increase the risk of sunburn, premature aging, eye damage, heat-related illnesses, and skin cancer.

Fortunately, many of these risks can be reduced through simple daily habits like wearing sunscreen, seeking shade, and using protective clothing.

Project SunProof exists to make those habits easier to understand—and easier to adopt.
